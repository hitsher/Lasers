Other laser systems:
  • Oxford Laser micromachining station with solid-state Nd: YAG (532/1064 nm) laser
  • ProMaster Optec laser micromachining station with excimer ArF (193 nm) laser
  • Trotec Speedy 300 engraving system, equipped with an air-cooler RF-excited pulsed CO2 laser (λ = 10.6 μm) Synrad, Inc.

Research

We deal with laser micro-processing since 2007.
We work in the field of:
  • laser color marking
  • manufacturing of electrical microstructures, mechanical or opto-mechanical - mikroantennas, metamaterials structures , RFID, MEMS, MEOMS
  • performance elements for microfluidics
  • biomedical applications - microreactors, lab-on-Chip, structuring of the surface
  • implant manufacturing (machining difficult materials with traditional methods)
  • manufacture of tools for microsurgery (stents, microsieves)
  • machining (micro) Ceramics: LTCC, AlN, Al2O3, zirconia, etc.
  • surface cleaning using laser ablation
  • cutting of composite materials, flexible, cutting sterile
  • study the composition of processed materials (LIBS)
  • new concepts (methodologies) micromachining
  • application of laser processing of new applications
  • contract research in collaboration with industry
